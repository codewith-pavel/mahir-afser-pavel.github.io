This work presents SLGRA-Net, a structure-aware latent graph reasoning network for 3D MRI brain tumor segmentation. The model aggregates 3D feature patches into graph nodes to capture long-range structural dependencies, while uncertainty-aware supervision helps address class imbalance and ambiguous boundary cases, particularly for the enhancing tumor region.
