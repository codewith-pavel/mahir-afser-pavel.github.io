This work proposes CLKD-MED, a cross-lingual knowledge distillation framework designed for clinical outcome modeling in low-resource languages. It combines prompt-conditioned neural translation, teacher-student distillation, and SHAP-based interpretability to improve transfer performance on clinical text while preserving efficiency. The method is evaluated on the MIMIC-III database for Bengali and reports strong AUC performance for length-of-stay and mortality prediction.
